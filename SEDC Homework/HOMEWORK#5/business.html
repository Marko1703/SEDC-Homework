Apple Reports Record Profits on iPhone Sales

Apple reported record profits of $94.7 billion in the fourth quarter of 2023, driven by strong sales of the iPhone 14 lineup. The company also saw growth in its services business, which includes Apple Music, Apple TV+, and the App Store.

Microsoft Announces $68.7 Billion Activision Blizzard Acquisition

Microsoft announced plans to acquire Activision Blizzard for $68.7 billion in cash. The deal, which is the largest in video game history, is expected to close in 2024. The acquisition will give Microsoft control of popular franchises like Call of Duty, World of Warcraft, and Candy Crush.

Meta Faces Antitrust Lawsuit Over Alleged Monopoly

Meta, the parent company of Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp, is facing an antitrust lawsuit from the Federal Trade Commission (FTC). The FTC alleges that Meta has engaged in anticompetitive behavior, such as acquiring rivals and copying their features, in order to maintain its monopoly power.
