US GDP Growth Slows to 1.9% in Q3

The US economy grew at an annual rate of 1.9% in the third quarter of 2023, down from 2.6% in the previous quarter. The slowdown was driven by a decline in consumer spending and business investment.

Unemployment Rate Holds Steady at 3.7%

The unemployment rate remained unchanged at 3.7% in October 2023, matching the lowest level since 1969. The job market continues to be strong, with employers adding an average of 261,000 jobs per month over the past three months.

Core Inflation Tickles Up to 2.3% in October

Core inflation, which excludes volatile food and energy prices, rose to 2.3% in October 2023, from 2.2% in September. The increase was driven by higher prices for housing, medical care, and transportation.
